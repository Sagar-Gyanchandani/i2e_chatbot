WEBINAR

Automate Conversion of Product and BATCH files to iVEDA approved format.

On 18th February 2021 Thursday 7:00 PM IST 

Register NowCompanies are entrusted upon the responsibility to move their data from the existing DAVA portal to iVeda by 31st March 2021. With business being severely hit by the pandemic, things are not going at a pace originally planned.  

In this webinar, members of the development team will describe how i2e Consulting is addressing the common concerns of converting existing Product and BATCH files in DAVA portal to iVeda approved format (XML Schema Files), along with bulk upload operations. The team will demonstrate how the system designed specifically for this purpose can accelerate your migration process with automated data extraction and processing.
