i2e Consulting is recognized by Great Place To Work India among India's Best Workplaces in IT & IT-BPM 2022! - i2e Consulting                                

November 30, 2022                                

i2e Consulting has been recognized by Great Place to Work® India among – India’s Best Workplaces in IT & IT-BPM 2022! GPW survey is India’s largest study on workplaces and is based on confidential employee feedback and an audit of the management processes.   
“We are delighted to be among the top 100 organizations in the IT & IT-BPM category. Being an IT company, it is an honor to get this recognition. At i2e people are our permanent assets, and we constantly work to improve our practices to attract the right talent and nurture them to become leaders.” Says Sudhir Karanth, CEO, of i2e Consulting. 
“i2e is fortunate to have a fantastic team whose hard work and dedication brought about great laurels for the company. The management always strives to bring in policies that provide the team with the freedom and space for individual growth,” says Vishal Rane, COO, of i2e Consulting. 
About The Great Place to Work®  
The Great Place to Work® Assessment is considered a ‘Gold Standard’ in workplace Culture assessment and no jury or individual can influence the results of the assessment. The recognition is based on a rigorous assessment independently conducted by Great Place to Work® Institute India.  
About i2e Consulting    
Formed in 2008, i2e Consulting is a software services company delivering both consultancy services and tailored technology solutions for organizations in the life sciences industry.  
To know more about the company’s culture and what the employees think of i2e browse through our website or get in touch with us.
