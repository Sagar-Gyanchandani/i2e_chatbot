Optimizing Resource Management for accurate reports and faster decision-making

Date: 19th October 2022    Time: 11:00 AM EST 

Pharma projects are complex and tend to stretch for a long duration, this makes resource management critical for the success of the projects. Moreover, the multi-country, multi-vendor environment demands accuracy and speed when it comes to managing resource allocation, tracking performance, etc. 


Automating resource management, building a centralized database, and using AI and ML for insights is the way to keep up with the growing demands. Join our experts as they elaborate more on resource management problems in the life science space and discuss the ideal approach for efficient resource management.
In this webinar you will learn:




Current resource management challenges in life sciences

Utilizing technology for resource management 


Automated reporting for accuracy and saving time using RPA 


Portfolio decision- making using AI and ML tools 


Real life success stories 

Who should attend?



Project managers in biotech and pharma, leaders of biotech and pharmaceutical industries. Portfolio and Project Management organizations, PMO Managers, and leaders of biotech and pharma R&D organizations.
