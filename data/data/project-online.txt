Project Online Reporting Bundle 



Project Online provides deep insights into resource utilization trends, supporting efficient project execution and informed resource management, all in one place. Power BI offers powerful reporting capabilities, such as Program Dashboards, Clinical and Tox Studies, Objectives, and KPIs. These reports give real-time insights into project progress, and risk assessment helping stakeholders make informed decisions. 



i2e excels in constructing a comprehensive reporting package that seamlessly integrates Project Online and Power BI, enhancing the efficiency and depth of project analysis. 



















i2e's R&D PPM Framework with Microsoft Project Online 



Leveraging our extensive domain expertise and a rich history of collaborating with pharmaceutical organizations, we formulated the R&D PPM Framework with Microsoft Project Online.  



This framework simplifies project oversight by combining planning, execution, and monitoring tools. For clinical studies, it aids in trial planning and execution, patient screening tracking, and resource management. Our R&D PPM framework also offers Resource Demand Forecasting and Capacity Planning features and robust reporting capabilities through Microsoft Power BI for real-time insights and informed decision-making. This includes detailed management dashboards for programs, clinical and tox studies, patient screening, and enrolment progress monitoring.
